    How to Play

    1. The computer will generate a 4 digit secret number, ranging from 1-9. Duplicates are not allowed.
    2. The player will then try to guess the computer's number who will then give the number of matches. If the matching digits are in their right positions, they are "bulls". But if they are in different positions, they are "cows".

    How to Win

    Reveal the computer's secret number in the fewest possible moves you can! The game is complete once the guess contains 4 bulls and 0 cows.


    Example of Game Play

    • Secret number: 1234
    • Player's guess: 4731
    • Answer: 1 bull and 2 cows. The bull is the 3, while the cows are 1 and 4.
