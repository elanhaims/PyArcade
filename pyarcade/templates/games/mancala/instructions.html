    How to Play

    1. Decide who plays first. Players will alternate turns with Player 1 first (the one sending the game invite) and Player 2 next (the one receiving an invite).
    2. The larger pockets are called stores or your own Mancala. Each player has one of their own. Player 1 (the one sending the request) has the store on the righthand side, while Player 2 (the one receiving game invite) will get the store on the lefthand side.
    3. On your turn, pick up all of the pieces in any one of the 12 (twelve) pockets by clicking on the pocket that you would like to distribute.
    4. The pieces will be distributed one by one along each of the 12 pockets counter-clockwise. If you reach your own store, a piece will be placed inside. If you reach your opponent's store, you will move onto the next pocket instead.
    5. If the last piece you drop is in your own Mancala, you get to take another turn.
    6. If the last piece you drop in is in an empty pocket, you get to retrieve that piece and all the pieces in the opposite pocket.
    7. All captured pieces will be in your Mancala.
    8. The game ends when all six pockets on one side of the Mancala board is empty.
    9. The player who has pieces on his/her side of the board (Player 1 owns bottom 6 pockets, Player 2 owns top 6 pockets) when the game ends also captures those pieces.
    10. Count all the pieces Player 1 and Player 2 own.

    How to Win

    If you are the player with more pieces than your opponent at the end of the game, you win!
